George Lucas

The man behind a galaxy far far away

Here's a time line of George Lucas' life:

  • 1944 - Born in Modesto, CA
  • 1971 - Founds Lucasfilm LTD.
  • 1977 - "Star Wars" is released
  • 1980 - "The Empire Strikes Back" is released
  • 1983 - Lucas creates THX and "Return of the Jedi" is released
  • 1999 - "The Phantom Menace" Released
  • 2002 -"Attack of the Clones" is released
  • 2005 - "Revenge of the Sith" Released
  • 2012 - Lucas sells the rights of "Star Wars" to the Disney Coorperation for $4 Billion
